Privacy Policy

DealWise Rental Investment Analyzer

Effective Date:

Summary: DealWise operates entirely within your browser and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All data processing occurs locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

No Personal Data Collection: DealWise does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or property data to our servers or any third parties.

Local Storage Only

The extension may store the following information locally on your device using Chrome's storage API:

  • User Preferences: Your customized investment parameters (mortgage rates, down payment percentages, management fees, etc.)
  • Extension Settings: Display preferences and configuration options

This information is stored only on your local device and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no information to use, process, or analyze. The extension operates entirely within your browser environment.

Local Data Processing

  • Property data is extracted from Zillow pages you visit and processed locally
  • Investment calculations are performed entirely within your browser
  • No data leaves your device or is shared with any external services

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

No Data Sharing: We do not share, sell, rent, or disclose any information to third parties because we do not collect any personal data in the first place.

4. Data Security

Since all processing occurs locally on your device:

  • No data transmission occurs, eliminating transmission security risks
  • Your data is protected by your device's existing security measures
  • Chrome's built-in security features provide additional protection

5. Third-Party Services

DealWise does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or external data sources. The extension operates independently within your browser environment.

6. Children's Privacy

DealWise is designed for real estate investment analysis and is not intended for use by children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

8. Your Rights and Choices

Since we do not collect personal data, traditional data subject rights (access, correction, deletion) do not apply. However, you can:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time to remove all locally stored preferences
  • Clear your browser's extension data to reset all stored settings
  • Disable the extension without losing any personal information

9. International Data Transfers

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there are no international data transfers to consider.
